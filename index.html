About the Artist

Hi, I'm Gennarino. Most people call me G. I'm a 25-year-old emerging artist based in the East Village, NYC.

I work across a range of mediums, including pencil, charcoal, acrylic, and watercolor, and I'm constantly experimenting with new tools and techniques. My work is often rooted in surrealistic scenes, especially those involving animals, while I continue to challenge myself by exploring more dramatic and expressive landscapes.

My passion for art began early at Preschool of the Arts, where creativity shaped how I see the world. Since then, I've continued to develop my practice through competitions, commissions, and personal exploration, with the long-term goal of exhibiting my work in a gallery.

Living in New York City is a constant source of inspiration. The people, energy, and unpredictability of the city influence both the subject and emotion behind my work.

Through my art, I aim to spark curiosity, challenge perspective, and inspire others to create.

My biggest inspiration is my mom, an architect with a lifelong dedication to her own artistic practice.

Outside of art, I enjoy staying active, trying new things, and meeting new people.
