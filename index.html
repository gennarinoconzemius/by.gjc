About

Gennarino Conzemius is a New York City–based artist whose work bridges realism, surrealism, and contemporary culture. With a style defined by bold contrast, expressive detail, and a balance between tradition and modernity, his art transforms everyday moments and pop influences into striking visual narratives.


Originally a Division 1 athlete turned creative, Gennarino brings discipline, intensity, and curiosity to his practice. Living and creating in New York City, he draws inspiration from its energy—its rhythm of solitude and community, its tension between order and chaos.

Achievements

  • Multiple-time Big East finalist
  • National team member for Luxembourg
  • School record holder in breaststroke events
